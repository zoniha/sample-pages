イヤホンジャックの向こう側

Album

COCOA's 134th album. The cover design was done by the color pencil artist Katahira Shunshi, and the collaboration of music and drawing was realized.

Includes 6 songs including "Kimi no Uta", the song used in the HONDA CARS Shizuoka TV commercial tie-in, and 3 new songs written entirely by the artist.
Please enjoy the world that unfolds on the other side of the earphone jack.
